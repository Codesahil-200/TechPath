Your Journey to Mastery

While every path is unique, most IT careers follow a similar progression of growth and responsibility.

1

Junior / Associate

Focus on learning core skills, fixing bugs, and understanding the codebase. You work under supervision.

2

Mid-Level

You own features, mentor juniors, and make architectural decisions. You work independently.

3

Senior

You design systems, solve complex problems, and drive technical strategy. You are a go-to expert.

4

Lead / Principal

You lead teams, define roadmaps, and align technology with business goals. You shape the culture.
